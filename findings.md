## Audience Research

### Primary Persona
- **Archetype:** The Successful Seeker
- **Demographics:** 35–55, likely entrepreneurial or senior-level professional
- **Pain:** External success without internal meaning; feeling "stuck at the ceiling"
- **Desire:** Breakthrough, soul alignment, deeper purpose, authentic self-expression
- **Awareness Level:** Problem-aware to solution-aware (NOT unaware)
- **Language that resonates:** "soul," "initiation," "breakthrough," "visionary," "transformation," "inner work," "awakening," "calling"
- **Language to avoid:** hype, overnight-results, corporate-speak, fear-based urgency

### Conversion Psychology Notes
- This audience is sophisticated — they've seen plenty of sales pages and are allergic to manipulation
- Trust and credibility must be established before the ask
- Transformation stories / social proof carry heavy weight
- Specificity beats vague promises
- The process and "what happens" must be made clear — they need to understand what they're stepping into

## Tech Stack Research

### CMS Options Considered

| CMS | Pros | Cons | Fit |
|-----|------|------|-----|
| Sanity.io | Flexible schema, great DX, free tier | Slight learning curve | ⭐ Strong |
| Payload CMS | Self-hosted, full TypeScript, built-in auth | Requires own DB | ⭐ Strong |
| Contentful | Mature, great API | Expensive at scale | OK |
| Strapi | Open source, flexible | Slower, heavier | OK |

### Frontend Options Considered

| Framework | Pros | Cons | Fit |
|-----------|------|------|-----|
| Next.js 14+ | App router, SSR/SSG, huge ecosystem | Complexity | ⭐ Strong |
| Astro | Ultra-fast, content-focused | Less dynamic | Good for pure landing |
| Remix | Great UX patterns, progressive | Smaller ecosystem | OK |
